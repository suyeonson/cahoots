Venting about people who misuse the word "literally"

Project Overview:
The word "figuratively" is used to indicate that a phrase is to be taken as a figure of speech, not literally. However, people have increasingly overused and misused the word "literally" to mean "figuratively," perverting the word's original meaning. Ever get annoyed when you hear people say things like, "That concert was so good I was literally dying"? Come find me in Kafein and we'll vent about our frustrations with people who are "literally the worst."
